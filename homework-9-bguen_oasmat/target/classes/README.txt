The file uscities.csv is derived from the simplemaps Basic United States Cities
Database, which can be found at https://simplemaps.com/data/us-cities and is
distributed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 License.

We removed the boroughs of New York City (Queens, Brooklyn, Bronx, Manhattan,
and Staten Island), whose population was already counted within New York [City],
and we removed cities outside the continental US (San Juan) because driving
distance was not meaningful.
